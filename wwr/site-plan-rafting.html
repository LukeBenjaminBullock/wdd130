Overview

Purpose

The great rivers of Colorado are becoming less and less popular over time. Unfortunately we are seeing a change in the activity levels of people. The invention of bolth the computer and the iphone tends to keep people occupied indoors. The purpose of this site is to inspire people to get back out into the world and enjoy those rampaging rivers once again.

Audience

In the building of this site we want to appeal to audiences old and young. Those who are new to the sport and those who have not visited it in quite some time. Of course we will want to target people with enough money to invest in the hobby, so households and athletes would make the most sense as a target audience. Maybe middle class individusls. Our content should be family oriented, showing happy smiles and joy of being outside. I think beautiful landscapes would appeal well, as well as a energetic color pallet. I expect the audience to normally use a desktop or laptop to access the site, but we shouldnt exclude mobile compatability.
