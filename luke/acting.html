Ever since I first got the opportunity to sing, I loved the way it made your whole body feel. Almost like you lighting up inside. And being on stage is a wonderful thing. Like my drama teacher said its called a "play" for a reason. I have been in three big roles up until this point.

Mathew Cutbert

Played role at age 16

From Anne of Green Gables.

Mathew cutbert is a kind shy old gentleman. In the first few scenes of the play he has requested a boy for adoption so that they might be able to help on the farm. Instead the girl, Anne (Spelled with an e) is waiting there at the train station. Mr. Cutbert is instantly drawn into the fantastical world of fantasy that the girl has created. And asks Marilla that they might be able to keep the child. Despite the old woman's protests the girl gets to stay, and green gables is never the same again.

Captain stache

Played role at age 18

From Peter pan and the star catcher.

When Fruin the drama teacher was giving out roles for the play Peter pan and the starcatcher. He warned those aditioning that there really wasn't any such thing as a main role in the play. That is there were no main roles other than captain stache. This character is a rather energetic and comical younger version of captain hook, and to play him well re quires a massive amount of energy, not to mention memorizing a small book. I remember running around the stage screaming in fear, face turning red with pain, and delivering long flowing nonsensacle monologues to the audience. All in all this character was a blast, and I'm not sure Ill ever be able to play something quite so exilerating.

Lord strout

Played role at age 21

From The lamp lighter

Out of all the characters I've played, this one certainetly has the darkest soul. One of the characters in the play has the tragic backstory of having had lord strout as his uncle. With this in mind, the sinister lord represents true darkness in the play. He is the absence of love and hates the concept with a burning passion. Beliving that the only true powers in the world are the primal instics that drive human behavior. Insticts such as Lust and fear. This is also the only costume where I supplied the whole peice.
