Site Plan

When I say that I am proud to be an artist is not to say that I am better than the next guy over. I think that if anything those who persu art are intelligent beyond compare, after all thats in a way the point of making art. To communicate the true colors of your mind.

This websight itself is something I would call a work of art. Its something I want to watch improve and grow as I add to it over time. I'll add it to my pile of pet projects.

Be it acting, sculpting, singing, or writing. I am a young budding artist. My talents are still developing. So when you spend your time here looking at my haphazard and sometimes unfinished projects. I would like to communicate the potential of my works more than the current products. Whenever I have deadlines to make, or an audience to please I finish my projects. Given ample time.

What aspects of your imagination do you want to bring forward? Is there any way I could help?
